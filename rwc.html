Rugby World Cup

Yes, the Rugby World Cup final is on 31 October 2015. No, the wedding is not on at the same time as the final.
Scroll down for more details...

The Rugby World Cup final kicks off at 16:00, Saturday 31 October GMT. This works out to 5:00, Sunday 1 November NZDT. What do you need to do if you want to watch the game?

  • SKY TVhas the broadcasting rights to the 2015 RWC. If you are staying in a hotel that night, the safest option is to check your accommodation has SKY.
  • If you have an existing Sky subscription and are staying in accommodation without Sky, checkout how you can access Sky TV online or from your phone at
  • If you don't have an existing Sky subscription and are convinced the All Blacks will be in the RWC final, Prime TV will be broadcasting every All Blacks match and highlights from each match day.
  • If you want to watch the final at a bar, there is a high chance bars in Auckland will be showing the game. Unfortunately we can't guarantee Waiheke Island bars will do the same.
  • And if you want to buy official All Blacks merchandise, visit www.champions.co.nz
